INEC-IPC

INEC | 2019(Jun) - 2020(feb)

The Índice de Precios al Consumidor (IPC) is a statistical indicator that measures the variation in the prices of groceries and services representative of the consumption of Costa Rican households, in a given period of time. This indicator is a source of information to estimate inflation, update monetary policy and, in general, for the economic planning of the country. It is also used in salary adjustments, readjustment of rental contracts, calculation of rate models, to estimate the change in purchasing power, among others.

INEC-ENUT

INEC | 2019(jan) - 2019(may)

The Encuesta Nacional de Uso del Tiempo (ENUT), provides statistical inputs necessary to make visible all forms of work of people, both outside and inside the home, whether they are paid or not. The data presented and the information that can be obtained through the development of indicators make it possible to monitor the gaps in the use of time by women and men in the different daily tasks, whether in the areas of work, community and family life.
